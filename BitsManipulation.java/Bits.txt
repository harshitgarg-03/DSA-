Formula of left shift (<<)
suppose a << b = a*2^b
like ex => 5 << 2 = 5*2^2 = 20


Formula of right shift (>>)
suppose a >> b = a/2^b
like ex => 6 >> 1 = 6/2^1 = 3

Setting of ith bit

Clear of ith bit
